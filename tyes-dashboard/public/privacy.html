Privacy Policy

Your data, explained — no legalese fog.

Trust is the foundation of everything we build. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. If a term is capitalized and not defined here, it has the meaning given in our Terms of Service.

1. Data Controller

1.1 "Controller" means Beauty by Sheika SRL, CUI 42348249, located in Mangalia, Constanta, Romania.

1.2 Contact: office@tyes.app

2. What We Collect

2.1 Account Information

Name, email address, authentication details (including Google OAuth identifiers), and password (hashed — never stored in plain text).

2.2 Billing Information

Billing name, address, company name, CUI, and J number for B2B invoicing. Payment card details are processed directly by Stripe and never touch our servers.

2.3 Creative Inputs and Outputs

Briefs, product photos, references, uploaded files, and generated images. We store this content to power features like order history, re-downloads, and revisions.

2.4 Device and Usage Data

IP address, approximate location, browser type, device info, pages visited, and time spent — collected automatically via essential analytics only.

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 Service Delivery

To create, deliver, and manage your Deliverables. Legal basis: contract performance.

3.2 Payments and Invoicing

To process payments via Stripe and generate fiscal invoices via SmartBill, including e-Factura submission to ANAF. Legal basis: legal obligation under Romanian tax law.

3.3 Communication

Delivery notifications, revision updates, and order confirmations. Legal basis: contract performance.

3.4 Improvement

To fix bugs and improve the platform. Legal basis: legitimate interest.

3.5 Legal Compliance

To meet tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements. Legal basis: legal obligation.

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Service Providers

Stripe (payment processing) — Stripe, Inc., USA. EU-US Data Privacy Framework certified.

Supabase (hosting, database, auth) — Supabase, Inc., USA/EU. EU data centers where available.

SmartBill (invoicing) — SmartBill SRL, Romania. Fiscal invoices and e-Factura to ANAF.

Resend (transactional email) — order confirmations and delivery notifications only.

Vercel (hosting) — Vercel, Inc., USA. Website hosting and CDN.

4.2 What We Don’t Do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not use it for marketing without your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Account data: duration of your account + 30 days after deletion.

5.2 Orders and images: 12 months after delivery (re-downloads, support).

5.3 Invoicing and billing: 10 years (Romanian tax and accounting law).

5.4 Technical logs: 90 days (security and debugging).

6. Your Rights

6.1 What You Can Do

Access. Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Rectification. Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

Erasure. Ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention).

Restriction. Ask us to limit how we process your data.

Portability. Receive your data in a structured, common format.

Object. Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

Withdraw consent. Where processing is consent-based, withdraw it anytime.

6.2 How to Exercise Them

Email us at office@tyes.app. We respond within 30 days. Not satisfied? You can lodge a complaint with the Romanian data protection authority (ANSPDCP) at www.dataprotection.ro.

7. Security

TLS/HTTPS encryption in transit, encrypted database storage, strict access controls, regular security reviews, and secure authentication (hashed passwords, OAuth 2.0).

8. International Transfers

Some providers are US-based. We ensure adequate safeguards: EU-US Data Privacy Framework (Stripe), Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), and EU data center selection where available.

9. Children

tyes is not directed to anyone under 18. If we learn we’ve collected data from a child, we’ll delete it promptly.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes are posted on our website. We’ll notify registered users of material changes via email.

11. Contact

Questions about your data? office@tyes.app.
