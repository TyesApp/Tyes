Cookie Policy

What cookies we use and why — spoiler: only the essentials.

We keep it simple. tyes uses only essential cookies — the ones that make our website actually work. No tracking. No ads. No surprises.

1. What Are Cookies

1.1 The Short Version

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize you and keep things like your login status working smoothly.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Only

Every cookie listed below is strictly necessary. We don’t use marketing, advertising, or non-essential tracking cookies.

Cookie Type Duration Purpose
sb-auth-token Essential Session / 7d Keeps you logged in (Supabase auth).
sb-refresh-token Essential 7 days Refreshes your session seamlessly.
__stripe_mid Essential 1 year Stripe fraud prevention at checkout.
cookie_consent Essential 1 year Remembers your cookie preference.

3. Third-Party Cookies

3.1 Stripe

Our payment processor may set its own cookies during checkout for fraud prevention. These are essential. We don’t embed social widgets, ad pixels, or third-party tracking.

4. Managing Cookies

4.1 Your Options

Because these cookies are essential, disabling them would break core functionality like login and payments. You can always delete cookies through your browser settings.

5. Changes

If we ever introduce non-essential cookies (analytics, marketing), we’ll update this policy and add a proper opt-in/opt-out mechanism.
